About Home Science Association

School Life

Our Philosophy

At Home Science Association Nursery and Primary School, we believe that every child is unique with their own strengths, interests, and learning style. Our approach to education is child-centered, focusing on developing the whole child - intellectually, socially, emotionally, and physically.

Founded in 1965, our school has a rich history of academic excellence and innovation. Our dedicated teachers bring learning to life through creative teaching methods and individualized attention.

We maintain small class sizes to ensure each child receives the attention they deserve, creating a safe and nurturing environment where children feel comfortable taking risks and exploring new ideas.

Admission Process

We welcome applications from families who share our educational philosophy and are looking for a nurturing environment for their children.

Our admission process is designed to ensure that Home Science Association is the right fit for your child and family. We consider each application carefully, looking beyond academic readiness to a child's curiosity, character, and potential.

Complete the inquiry form to start your journey with us. Our admissions team will contact you to schedule a school tour and provide more information about the next steps.
